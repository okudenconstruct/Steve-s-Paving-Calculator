Reference Calculations

  • Volume Calculations:
  •   • Excavation Volume (CY): (Area in SY × Depth in inches) ÷ 324
  •   • DGA Volume (CY): (Area in SY × Depth in inches) ÷ 324
  • Tonnage Calculations:
  •   • DGA Tons: Volume in CY × 1.9
  •   • Milling Tons: Area in SY × Depth in inches × 0.0575
  •   • Base Course Tons: Area in SY × Depth in inches × 0.0575
  •   • Surface Course Tons: Area in SY × Depth in inches × 0.0575
  • Days Calculations:
  •   • Excavation Days: Volume in CY ÷ Daily Rate in CY/day, rounded up to nearest 0.5
  •   • DGA Days: Volume in CY ÷ Daily Rate in CY/day, rounded up to nearest 0.5
  •   • Milling Days: Area in SY ÷ Daily Rate in SY/day, rounded up to nearest 0.5
  •   • Paving Days: Area in SY ÷ Daily Rate in SY/day, rounded up to nearest 0.5
  • Truck Fleet Calculations:
  •   • Daily Loads = Daily Volume ÷ 16 CY/truck (excavation/DGA) OR Daily Tons ÷ 22 tons/truck (asphalt)
  •   • Trucks per Day = (Daily Loads × Travel Time in minutes) ÷ 480 minutes/day
  •   • Total Truck Hours = Trucks per Day × Total Days × 8 hours/day
  • Truck Capacities:
  •   • Asphalt Materials: 22 tons per truck (milling, base, surface)
  •   • Volume Materials: 16 CY per truck (excavation, DGA)
  • Travel Time Logic:
  •   • Includes 40 minutes total for loading (20 min) and unloading (20 min)
  •   • Longer travel times require more trucks to maintain continuous production
  •   • 480 minutes = 8-hour workday for continuous operation
  • Conversion Factor:
  •   • 324 = 9 sq ft/SY × 36 inches/yard (standard SY to CY conversion)
